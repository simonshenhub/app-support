Privacy Policy

WristFlow — Hydration Tracker

Overview

WristFlow is designed with your privacy in mind. Your health data stays on your device and is never sent to any server.

Data We Access

WristFlow reads and writes the following data through Apple HealthKit:

  • Water Intake (read & write) — to log and display your daily hydration
  • Workouts (read only) — to send you a hydration reminder after you finish a workout

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using Apple HealthKit and on-device preferences. WristFlow does not have a server, database, or cloud service. Your data never leaves your device.

Data Sharing

WristFlow does not share, sell, or transmit any of your data to third parties. There are no analytics, no tracking, and no advertising SDKs.

Notifications

WristFlow may send local notifications to remind you to drink water. These are generated entirely on your device and are not sent through any external service. You can disable them at any time in the app settings or in iOS Settings.

Third-Party Services

WristFlow does not use any third-party services, analytics, crash reporting, or advertising frameworks.

Children's Privacy

WristFlow does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new effective date.
