Privacy Policy

StepPeek — Step Counter

Overview

StepPeek is designed with your privacy in mind. Your health data stays on your device and is never sent to any server.

Data We Access

StepPeek reads the following data through Apple HealthKit:

  • Step Count (read only) — to display your daily steps
  • Walking + Running Distance (read only) — to show distance traveled
  • Active Energy Burned (read only) — to display calories burned

StepPeek does not write any data to HealthKit.

Data Storage

All data is read from Apple HealthKit on your device. StepPeek does not have a server, database, or cloud service. Your data never leaves your device.

Data Sharing

StepPeek does not share, sell, or transmit any of your data to third parties. There are no analytics, no tracking, and no advertising SDKs.

Third-Party Services

StepPeek does not use any third-party services, analytics, crash reporting, or advertising frameworks.

Children's Privacy

StepPeek does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new effective date.
