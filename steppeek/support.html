Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add the complication to my watch face?

Long-press your watch face, tap Edit, swipe to the complications page, tap a slot, and select StepPeek from the list.

Why does StepPeek show 0 steps?

Make sure you've granted HealthKit access. Open StepPeek on your iPhone, tap "Grant HealthKit Access", and enable all permissions.

Where is my data stored?

StepPeek reads directly from Apple Health on your device. It has no server or cloud storage.
